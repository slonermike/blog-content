Lunchlady and Sloppy Joe are what run this site.

Every time I had to do anything custom in Wordpress, I...well, I just didn't. It was never neither fun nor rewarding. I knew that I'd never use 90% of what it did, and yet I was still paying for the overhead of wrestling with it to run my site.

Looking for a project that would expand on my existing knowledge of Node, React, and Redux, I set the whole thing up to be backed by static JSON and HTML, served up by lunchlady, a Node-based command-line tool.

Lunchlady will pull down the source of sloppy-joe and combine it with your site's data to generate a single-page app with dynamically-loaded content and theming.
