Have you head of Tower of Goo? No, I didn't make it. But when I found out where it came from, The Experimental Gameplay Project, I couldn't wait to throw my hat into the ring.

Per the rules of EGP, I spent 7 days from concept to completion making a game following the theme 'Night and Day.'

The game is a puzzle platformer built on my custom SlonArch engine. The graphics were hand-drawn with Sharpie, then photographed and colored in Photoshop.
