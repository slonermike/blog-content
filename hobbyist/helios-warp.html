In 2015, I built a Tron-inspired infinite runner game in Unity for iPhone -- Helios Warp. I worked with synthwave artists to feature their music, exchanging free use of their music in for driving traffic through the game to their music sharing sites.

In 2016, I reworked the game to run on a PC with custom arcade controls, and the game was housed in a local arcade for a month. All the proceeds went to a local charity. The game was again featured at the 2017 Northwest Pinball & Arcade Expo.

The iPhone version of the game is no longer available (I did not renew my Apple developer license), but if you are interested in playing, let me know -- the arcade version is playable on desktops.
