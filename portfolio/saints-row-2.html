From 2007-2008, I worked on Saints Row 2, which was my first project out of college.

I focused primarily on gameplay systems, side missions (Activities and Diversions), and occasionally, story missions, working closely with designers to convert a technical design document, to a playable prototype, and eventually to a polished, shippable state.

Saints Row 2 presented unique challenges in that all gameplay modes supported untethered co-op gameplay, further complicated by its use of a fully-featured rigid-body physics engine in a streaming, open world.

I worked on the following side missions, and in the case of Trail Blazing, provided significant design direction.

  • Fight Club
  • Septic Avenger
  • FUZZ
  • Trail Blazing
  • Chop Shop
  • Taxi Driver
  • Driving Stunts
  • Blackjack & Poker
  • Streaking
