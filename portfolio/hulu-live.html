Hulu with Live TV is an OTT delivery of both VOD and Digital MVPD (linear TV stream) content in a single experience -- providing extensive personalization, DVR capabilities, and wrangling an enormous content library into a uniform browse & playback experience.

In 2016-2017, I collaborated with Living Room team to develop a brand new Hulu app from nothing to fully-functional using CoffeeScript (and later TypeScript) in a single-page format with HTML5 and SASS visuals, and custom platform bindings for scrolling animations.

Since then, I have worked with product and client teams to carefully review and implement our evolving designs, working out ways to optimize the experience for all users -- maintaining a high-quality experience, regardless of device power.

I have also collaborated with the design and product teams to define our design patterns and developer needs for design documentation. This lead to a move toward a version-controlled component library, allowing design to continue working on in-progress designs while development implements fully-vetted previous versions.

I am the development lead for the preview program, which delivers early-access builds to Hulu employees and partners. This allows us to try new features in real-world scenarios and gather user feedback and error reporting for features which have not yet been released.
