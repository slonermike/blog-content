In 2011, I joined the team at Treyarch, and began work on the sequel to the hugely-successful Call of Duty: Black Ops.

I worked as both a designer and scripter for the Odysseus story mission, collaborating with nearly every discipline -- producers, artists, writers, programmers -- to deliver a top-notch cinematic gameplay experience.

Later in the project, I provided scripting support for the RTS-style Strike Force missions and the associated mission hub, built in the same environment as the Odysseus mission.
